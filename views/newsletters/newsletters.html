Volunteers Needed for Family Science Night @ LBJ 

SHPE Austin will be hosting Family Science Night (Noche De Ciencias), a bilingual event that targets Austin area families, to garner interest in STEM and provide valuable information on how to prepare students for a future in a STEM field.  There will be free food and many fun activities - so bring yourself and your family! In addition, SHPE Austin is looking for volunteers to help out the day of the event. Those interested can sign-up HERE. 
Date: April 7th (6:30-9:30PM) at Lyndon B. Johnson High School.

Volunteers Needed for Latinitas Code Chica Conference 2016

SHPE Austin seeks volunteers to help out at the Latinitas Code Chica Conference next month. This event will teach girls how to use media and technology to design, code and market their own mobile app idea. Roles we are looking to assist with include: workshop assistant, lunchtime crew, clean up crew, event historian, photographer, and more. If you would like to register a participant or donate a registration pass click HERE. Date: April 16th (9AM-4PM) at ACC Eastview Campus
